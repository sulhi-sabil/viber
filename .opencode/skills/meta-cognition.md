# Skill: Meta-Cognition

Think about thinking - analyze and improve reasoning processes.

## Commands

- Self-reflection: Review reasoning process used
- Strategy evaluation: Assess effectiveness of approaches taken
- Bias detection: Identify cognitive biases in decision making
- Process optimization: Improve thinking patterns

## Meta-Cognitive Questions

1. **Before Acting**
   - What approach am I considering?
   - Why is this approach appropriate?
   - What alternatives did I consider?
   - What could go wrong?

2. **During Execution**
   - Is this working as expected?
   - Should I adjust my approach?
   - Am I making assumptions?

3. **After Completion**
   - Did the approach work?
   - What would I do differently?
   - What did I learn?

## Cognitive Biases to Avoid

- Confirmation bias
- Overconfidence
- Anchoring
- Sunk cost fallacy
- Availability heuristic
